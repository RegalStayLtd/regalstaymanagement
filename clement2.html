Saint Clement's Street, Oxfordshire

Property Description

Your exquisite retreat in the historic city of Oxfordshire. Prepare to immerse yourself in a world of comfort and sophistication in this exceptional offering.

With two beautifully appointed bedrooms and a stylishly designed bathroom, this stunning property can accommodate a maximum of six discerning guests.

Located just a two-minute stroll from the iconic Oxford Botanic Garden, you'll have the opportunity to immerse yourself in the natural beauty and tranquility of the surroundings. Step out onto the vibrant High Street and discover a bustling hub of activity, offering a myriad of shops, cafes, and cultural attractions.

The prestigious University of Oxford is also just a short two-minute walk away, allowing you to delve into the rich academic history of the city. Euro Supermarket is conveniently located within a two-minute walk, ensuring easy access to all your grocery needs. Indulge in the vibrant atmosphere and delightful cuisine at James St. Tavern and Antep Kitchen, both just a few minutes away.

For nature enthusiasts, the scenic Rivermead Nature Park awaits, a leisurely eleven-minute stroll from your doorstep. Explore the lush greenery and serene surroundings, providing a welcome escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

This extraordinary property boasts a range of luxurious amenities to enhance your stay. Stay connected with fast Wi-Fi, allowing you to effortlessly browse and stream. Enjoy the convenience of self-check-in, providing flexibility and ease upon your arrival. The exclusive roof terrace outside offers a serene space to unwind and soak up the atmosphere of the city. The property comfortably sleeps up to six guests, ensuring ample space and comfort for everyone. Delight in entertainment with the smart TV, offering a wide selection of shows and movies for your enjoyment.

Book your stay today and indulge in a luxurious experience in the heart of Oxfordshire. Explore the city's cultural treasures, visit renowned landmarks, and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
