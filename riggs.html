High Riggs, Edinburgh

Property Description

Prepare to immerse yourself in a world of comfort and sophistication in this exceptional offering. Featuring one exquisitely designed bedroom and a stylishly appointed bathroom, this stunning property can accommodate a maximum of four discerning guests. Whether you're visiting with a loved one or a small group of friends, this spacious retreat ensures a memorable stay.

Discover the rich history and allure of Edinburgh with a range of attractions just minutes away. Embark on an eerie adventure with the City of the Dead Tours, located just a four-minute walk from the property. Immerse yourself in the fascinating history of Gladstone's Land, explore the majestic Edinburgh Castle, and indulge in the illusions and curiosities at Camera Obscura & World of Illusions and The Edinburgh Dungeon – all just a short distance away.

Nature lovers can enjoy the nearby Merchiston Park and Murieston Park, perfect for leisurely strolls and soaking up the picturesque surroundings. Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of the Royal Mile, just a ten-minute walk from your doorstep. Experience the bustling energy and eclectic shops at St James Quarter and Multrees Walk, both within easy reach.

This extraordinary property boasts a range of luxurious amenities to enhance your stay. The deluxe double bed in the bedroom and deluxe double sofa bed in the living room ensure a comfortable night's sleep for up to four guests. Unwind and enjoy entertainment with the flat HD TV in the living area, complete with Netflix for your viewing pleasure. Stay connected with free superfast WiFi, allowing you to effortlessly browse and stream.

Book your stay today and immerse yourself in a world of luxury in the heart of Edinburgh. Explore the city's rich heritage, visit iconic landmarks, and create cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
